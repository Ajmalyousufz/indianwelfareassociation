About Us

IWA INDIA FOUNATION , a non-governmental organisation founded by Dr PA Muhammed Farooq Naeemi in 2013, have set new standards in the field of charity work. IWA, since its inception, have been tirelessly working to ameliorate the living conditions of the people belonging to underprivileged and marginalised communities in India.
